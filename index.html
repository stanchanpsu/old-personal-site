About

Hello world, I'm Stan! Currently, I am a junior Chemical Engineering student at Penn State which makes me really excited, but I also enjoy many other things. Recently, I've found a hobby in programming and web development after auditing a class from Harvard. So, to practice my new coding skills, I made this website to share with you the things that I love. Other interests of mine include outreach and mentoring, karate, and peace & justice.

I've also had a few amazing work experiences including creating artificial photosynthesis, making toilet paper, and teaching lego robotics to children. When I'm not working or teaching, I enjoy learning to cook food or to hack software and electronics. Above all, I am happy being happy. To me, happiness is being passionate, in the moment, and living every day like I'm going back in time to relive it on purpose. I hope you can always do the same.
